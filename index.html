Here's A Time Line Of Bob Marley

  • 1945 - Bob Marley was born in St Ann Parish, Jamaica.

  • 1981 - Bob Marley died in Miami, Florida, surrounded by his family.

  • 1962 - Bob's mother remarried and moved to America, leaving him alone in Jamaica. He began to socialize with those of the Rastan faith, and although raised Catholic he adopted the Rastafari Religion.

  • 1963 - Bob Marley and the Wailers was formed when Bob Marley and Bunney Wailer met Peter Tosh. Originally a ska band, they evolved into a Jamacan reggae band over time.
  • 1966 - Haile Selassie, emperor of Ethiopia and worshipped by the Rastafari, visits Jamaica. Many, many Rastafari were present, including Bob.

  • 1976 - The People's National Party's (PNP) representative, Michael Manley, had a good relationship with Bob, which gained him support and admiration. After agreeing to perform a peace concert in Jamaica to try to settle down political tension, Bob's home was assaulted by armed men who shot at him, his wife, and his manager. He escaped with few injuries, and while both of the others recieved serious wounds, they all survived.

  • 1977 - Bob was diagnosed with melanoma in his toe. Though doctors recommended he amputate his entire leg, he followed the advice of his family and friends and only removed the infected part of the toe.

  • 1980 - Bob arrived in Zimbabwe to play a concert there, one encouraging the movement to liberate the black people there.Bob recevied the Medal of Peace from the United Nations.

  • 1981- Bob died in Miami, Florida from cancer that he had been fighting for four years. Though his treatment in Germany had allowed him to fight it for many months, it had become apparent that he didn't have long to live. He set out to visit Jamaica one last time, but unfortunately didn't make it.Bob's funeral was attended by more than 30,000 people, which was held in the National Arena in Kingston, Jamaica.
